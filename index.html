FAQ

Common questions.

Who has to comply?

Any brand selling to an EU shipping address — regardless of where your company is based. Even one EU shipment triggers the requirement.

What happens if I don't comply by June 19?

Up to 4% of your annual turnover per the directive. Enforcement is by each EU member state's consumer authority. No grace period.

How is this different from regular returns?

It's a separate, stricter flow: 14-day window, mandatory refund of shipping and duties, no return-condition requirements, no restocking fees. Most returns portals don't meet all the new rules.
